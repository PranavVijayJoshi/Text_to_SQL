Question:- How many different products has 'Pranav Joshi' purchased in total?
SQL:- SELECT COUNT(DISTINCT p.product_id) AS count_of_products 
FROM customers c 
INNER JOIN orders o ON c.customer_id = o.customer_id 
INNER JOIN order_items oi ON o.order_id = oi.order_id 
INNER JOIN products p ON oi.product_id = p.product_id 
WHERE LOWER(c.first_name) = 'pranav' AND LOWER(c.last_name) = 'joshi'
Answer:- Pranav Joshi has purchased 3 different products in total.

Question:- What is the total revenue generated by the 'Laptops' category?
Answer:- The total revenue generated by the 'Laptops' category is $3498.00.
SQL:- SELECT 
  SUM(oi.quantity * oi.unit_price) AS total_revenue
FROM 
  categories c
INNER JOIN 
  products p ON c.category_id = p.category_id
INNER JOIN 
  order_items oi ON p.product_id = oi.product_id
INNER JOIN 
  orders o ON oi.order_id = o.order_id
WHERE 
  LOWER(c.category_name) = 'laptops';

Question:-  For each city, find the customer who has spent the most money. Show the city, the customer name, and the amount.
Answer:- The customer who has spent the most money is Pranav Joshi from Pune, with a total spending of $2684.00.
SQL:- WITH customer_spending AS (
  SELECT 
    c.city, 
    c.first_name, 
    c.last_name, 
    SUM(oi.quantity * oi.unit_price) AS total_spent
  FROM 
    customers c
  INNER JOIN 
    orders o ON c.customer_id = o.customer_id
  INNER JOIN 
    order_items oi ON o.order_id = oi.order_id
  INNER JOIN 
    products p ON oi.product_id = p.product_id
  GROUP BY 
    c.city, 
    c.first_name, 
    c.last_name
)
SELECT 
  city, 
  first_name, 
  last_name, 
  total_spent
FROM (
  SELECT 
    city, 
    first_name, 
    last_name, 
    total_spent,
    ROW_NUMBER() OVER (PARTITION BY city ORDER BY total_spent DESC) AS row_num
  FROM 
    customer_spending
) subquery
WHERE 
  row_num = 1
ORDER BY 
  total_spent DESC
LIMIT 1;
